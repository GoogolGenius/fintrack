Help Center

FinTrack is your personal financial tracking tool. You can track income and expenses, set savings goals, view interactive charts, and manage your transaction history all in one place. With intuitive features and real-time insights, staying on top of your finances has never been easier.

Still have questions? Use our AI assistant to get quick, personalized help with anything related to FinTrack.

When adding a transaction, you can assign it to a category like Food, Transportation, or Income. This helps organize your finances and improve visualizations.

FinTrack includes a Visualization page with interactive bar and pie charts that break down your transactions by category and time period.

The History tab lets you view, search, and sort your past transactions. You can filter by category, date, and amount.

Go to the History tab, locate the transaction, and use the edit or delete icons to make changes or remove it entirely.

FinTrack lets you add both income and expense transactions, making it easy to monitor your cash flow and budgeting.

Exporting to CSV and JSON is supported. You can download your transaction history to use in spreadsheets or for backup purposes.

We prioritize your privacy. FinTrack stores your data securely and does not share it with third parties. All transactions are encrypted and tied to your account only.

FinTrack allows you to add and manage savings goals. Set a target amount and date, then track your progress over time directly from your dashboard.
